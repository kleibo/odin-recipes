Lasagna Recipe

Description

Lasagna is a favorite dish of garfield the cat. It is a wide, flat sheet of pasta, usually made with several layers of sheets, which can contain ingredients such as meat and cheese.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound
  • 4 tablespoons of butter
  • 1/4 cup of all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups of milk
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon of black pepper
  • 2 cups of shreeded cheddar cheese

Steps

  1. Cook the meat:Cook the ground meat in a skillet until browned and crumbly. Add the onion and continue cooking until it's translucent. Stir in the canned tomato products, half of the parsley, garlic, basil, 1.5 teaspoons of salt, oregano, and sugar.
  2. Cook the noodles: Boil the lasagna noodles in lightly salted water until they're al dente.
  3. Make the cheese layer: Mix cottage cheese, Parmesan cheese, eggs, the remaining parsley, the remaining salt, and pepper in a bowl.
  4. Assemble the lasagna: Layer the ingredients according to the recipe (starting with sauce and ending with mozzarella) until the lasagna is assembled.
  5. Bake the lasagna: Cover with foil and bake in the preheated oven for about half an hour. Remove the foil and continue baking until the top is golden brown.

Mac and Cheese

Description

Mac and cheese is a popular comfort food dish that typically consists of elbow macaroni pasta, a cheese sauce, and milk. It is a simple and delicious meal that is often served as a side dish, but can also be enjoyed as a main dish. The cheese sauce is typically made with a combination of cheddar and other cheeses, which gives the dish its rich, creamy flavor.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of ground beef
  • 1/2 onion, diced
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 can of crushed tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup of beef broth
  • 1 tablesppon of tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon of sugar
  • 1 teaspoon of dried basil

Steps

  1. Boil the noodles: Boil the macaroni in salted water until the noodles are al dente. Drain and transfer to a prepared baking dish.
  2. Make the cheese sauce: Melt butter, then whisk in the flour. Whisk in the milk, bring to a simmer, and stir in the cheeses. Season with salt and pepper and continue simmering until the sauce is thick. Pour the sauce over the noodles and stir.
  3. Make the topping: Melt two tablespoons of butter in a skillet, add the bread crumbs, and toast until the crumbs are brown. Spread the topping over the macaroni and cheese, then sprinkle with paprika.
  4. Bake the mac and cheese: Bake in the preheated oven until the topping is golden brown.
