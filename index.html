Mac and Cheese

Description

Mac and cheese is a popular comfort food dish that typically consists of elbow macaroni pasta, a cheese sauce, and milk. It is a simple and delicious meal that is often served as a side dish, but can also be enjoyed as a main dish. The cheese sauce is typically made with a combination of cheddar and other cheeses, which gives the dish its rich, creamy flavor.
